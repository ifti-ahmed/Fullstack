Tax cuts signal radical shift in No 10 policy

By Chris Mason
Political editor, BBC News
24 September




Wow.

A BIG political moment.

For something that wasn't even technically a Budget, it was one heck of a statement
of intent from the new prime minister and new chancellor.

And it is a gamble.

A massive leap in a new direction.

It amounts to the wholesale shredding of previous economic policy:
both the ones adopted by Conservative governments over the last 12 years (which Liz Truss sat within for most of that time)
and those of Labour before that.
